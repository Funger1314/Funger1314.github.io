About

My name is Yifang Qiu, and I am a student at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. I am interested in data science, information design, and how technology can help people share ideas more effectively. I built this website as part of the LIS 351 course to practice creating accessible, well-structured webpages. Through this project, I hope to learn how to combine usability, design, and storytelling in digital spaces.
